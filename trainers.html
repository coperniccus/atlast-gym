Meet Our Trainers

Nicole Bermundo

Bermundo's focus on the pricing section of the HTML ensures clarity and transparency for potential gym members. They meticulously merged separate HTML files, creating a seamless user experience as visitors navigate the website. Their careful work streamlines the process of finding pricing information, making it easy for users to compare plans and choose the one that best suits their needs.

Kerby Bongon

Bongon's contribution goes beyond the essential "Contact Us" section of the HTML. They also crafted the site map, serving as a roadmap for users to effortlessly find the information they seek. This user-friendly site map empowers visitors to explore the website efficiently, fostering a positive first impression.

Angela Cabanes

Cabanes played a multifaceted role, taking on the responsibility of both design and content. Her keen eye for design selected the perfect HTML template that serves as the foundation for the website's visual appeal. She also played a pivotal role in shaping the brand identity by creating the gym's name, logo, and color palette. These design choices establish a cohesive visual language that reflects the gym's personality and values.

Ronin Concepcion

Concepcion's focus on the "Programs" section of the HTML ensures that potential members have a clear understanding of the gym's offerings. Their contribution extends beyond simply listing the programs; they crafted compelling descriptions that capture the essence of each program and its potential benefits. Concepcion's creativity also brought the gym mascot, the Greek god Atlas, to life. Atlas embodies strength and endurance, perfectly aligning with the gym's mission. Furthermore, Concepcion penned most of the website's descriptive text, infusing the website with engaging and informative content.

Agatha Wendie Floreta

Floreta's expertise encompasses both web design and graphic design. They spearheaded the creation of the website's homepage, the first impression visitors encounter. Floreta's skillful use of HTML and their talent for design come together to create a visually appealing and informative homepage that entices visitors to explore further. Their proficiency in Photoshop likely played a role in crafting visual elements that enhance the website's overall aesthetic. Additionally, Floreta's creativity brought forth the gym's catchy catchphrase, a memorable tagline that encapsulates the gym's spirit.
